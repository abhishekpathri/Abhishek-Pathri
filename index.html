Brief Intro

I love to entertain, it makes me happy to be the reason for someones smile. Well known for my Parody Songs and Lyrical ventures. I stay humbled by the love, affection and encouragement of my well wishers. I have been a performer since my school days in Sri Sathya Sai Primary School, enacting dramas, playing for school band and doing my own creative mischief. Fun never ends.

My Hobbies

Being a big fan of music and AR Rahman sir I have always wanted to learn as many musical instruments as possible and I am learning everyday. I can play the Bansuri Flute, Keyboard, Alto, Recorder, Violin, Harmonica, Dholak and Kanjeera.

Education (because it is a must for everyone)

I have a dream/long term goal to set up an organization to educate the deprived and poor children with practical knowledge and related theory. I have earned a B.Tech Mechanical Engineering degree from K L University (2009-13) and I am currently pursuing my Masters in Mechanical Engineering at University of Massachusetts Dartmouth.
